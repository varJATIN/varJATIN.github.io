Days at IIT Roorkee.

  • May 15, 2018
  • In Lifestyle Education

There is a collections of sweet and sour memories inside my brain, some are also in heart(ya! i know it is not possible, Technically those are so, intense that my brain stop functioning whenever i try to remember those then I figured out that it is not the best place to keep these shits sorry! so I decided to throw them some where else in order to stop their execution and disruption of my brain.)

From now onwards my journey begins

After the training of one and half year I able to make it, but not able to make it as I want. At that point of time I had two options either prepare for one more year or be happy what I got. I refuse both of it and go for third one(wait third one!)(It is not from memories so, let me continue), first let me familarise you what was the third option, to get what you are not able to get, you have to compit with those because of whom you are not able to get what you want to get.(what the ****)(ya! first mistake of my engineering life)
Journey started on july 21 2017 as I enrolled for 4 years Btech programme in BioTechnology,Really how I will study biology for 4 years. We all have a orientation programme of one weak we have to go to convocation hall listen the talks and boring advices. I don't get any point for conducting it, because as a engineer we only listen to the seniors who have achivements.
I and all others stated struggling to correct what the kismat had did(did you believe)(they used to say that, not I) there are things that divert our focus from that so called branch change. Those who had stronger will can only stand against the rath of destiny. I used to go to reading room one the daily basis and wasting time in reading books and solving questions preparing for exams and all other boring stuffs literally my life was sucked.
And finally the destiny has arrived I got brance change to Civil Engineering and my friend able to make it from civil to electrical.

you may be thinking why I had not described that in detail because that shits are now not in my mind

Story continued...

I am not narrating it in sequencial manner these are the just snippets of my memories

Story started after the winter break of first semester. I am not good when it came to physical appearance so, i decided to gain some muscles, we have a very good central gym but not coed because college adminstration want less crowd in gym. As I am not of that kind so i made a schedule to wake up in the morning at 5:40 am (really! hahahahaha)(listen, I followed it)(but for how many day?)(for many... go back to your place and listen) and hit the gym. This legacy continue for two weeks then i take a break for one day. what, I was seroius for gym and continue it for 2 and half month in one semester some breaks are due to assigment submission dates, mid semster and end semester.
